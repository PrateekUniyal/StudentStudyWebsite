Why Study in Canada?

Certainly, Canada is a top study abroad destination for Indian students. The country provides high-quality education at reasonable costs, making it an attractive option for international students. Canadian institutions offer a vibrant student life, with many of them being ranked among the top 10 global universities. The Canadian Ministry of Education actively promotes the enrollment of foreign students and provides financial aid and facilities to support their stay in the country. Additionally, Canada's multicultural society ensures that students from India feel welcomed and integrated. The opportunity to gain work experience through post-graduation work permits and the possibility of applying for permanent residency further enhance Canada's appeal as a study destination...

Part time Careers and Work Opportunities in Canada

Work Opportunities

Starting in September 2024, international students will be allowed to work off-campus for up to 24 hours per week. During scheduled breaks, such as summer or winter holidays, they will be permitted to work full-time.

Post Study Work VISA

Beginning September 1, 2024, students enrolled in graduate programs and study programs under curriculum licensing agreements will not be eligible for a post-study work visa in Canada. However, students who complete master's programs in Canada will soon be eligible for three-year Post-Graduation Work Permits (PGWPs).
